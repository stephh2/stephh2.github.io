About Me

I was born in Galesburg, IL on Thanksgiving of 1996 but by Christmas, my family and I had returned to our home in Montego Bay, Jamaica. We all lived in Jamaica until August of 2004, when my mom got a new job and we moved to Lexington, KY. Lexington was home for 11 years but in August of 2015 I moved to Blacksburg, VA to attend Virginia Polytechnic Institute and State University.

At Virginia Tech it is common for students to say proudly, "This is Home" and it honestly has become just that. I got involved in many different clubs, groups, and activities that created a family like culture around me. I have been a sister in my social sorority, Gamma Delta Sigma, for 3 years and have even held the position of External Philanthropy chair. I play on an Intramural Softball team and am proud member of the Society of Women in Engineering. If these activities don’t keep me too busy, I enjoy hiking the Appalachian Trail with friends and cheering on the Hokies in all sporting events.

A hobby I hope to continue in my future is traveling. It could be around the US (probably going to MLB games), or around the world but I believe that traveling is one of the most important and underrated experiences in a person’s life. I enjoy seeing other cultures around the world and how they live, eat and explore differently than I do. So far, I have been to 15 countries across 4 continents and want to see even more. If given the opportunity, everyone should travel in their life.

Education

High School: Paul Laurence Dunbar High School

My High School had about 2,200 students and had several different tracks of education for the goals of each individual student. I chose to follow the track that included 5 AP courses and 1 Dual Enrollment course. By graduation in May of 2015, I had earned a 4.0 GPA and accrued 17 college credits which would later allow me to graduate college in only 3 and a half years.

College: Virginia Polytechnic Institute and State University

I am about to start my 4th year at Virginia Tech and am excited to graduate in December. I will graduate with my Bachelors of Science in Computer Engineering. I currently have a GPA of 3.6 and have been on the Dean's List 5 of my 6 completed semesters.

Self-Education

These courses include a Web Development and a React Native App Developement class. In this process, I have been able to teach myself how to create this website and soon make the apps I want that aren't available to me.
